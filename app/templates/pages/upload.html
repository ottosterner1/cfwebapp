CSV Format Guide

Your CSV should include the following columns:

  • student_name (required)
  • age (required)
  • forehand (YES/NEARLY/NOT YET)
  • backhand (YES/NEARLY/NOT YET)
  • movement (YES/NEARLY/NOT YET)
  • overall_rating (1-5)
  • next_group_recommendation
  • notes (optional)
